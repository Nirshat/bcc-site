About Us

History: The Binalatongan Community College (BCC) is owned and operated by the City Government of San Carlos for the people of San Carlos City and its nearby towns. It was a verbal agreement between the late City Mayor Julian V. Resuello and Engr. Marcelo M. Casillan Jr., Chairman of the Casillan Foundation in May 2006 that Binalatongan Community College in San Carlos City, Pangasinan will be established.

On July 24, 2006, City Ordinance No. 38-06 was passed by the Members of the Sangguniang Panglungsod of the City of San Carlos providing for the establishment of a community College to be known as "Binalatongan Community College". BCC maintained its students and faculty at the central I School Complex, Roxas Boulevard, on its own facilities. In 2010, BCC transferred to its own building at Barangay Ilang, San Carlos City. Up to the present, BCC continues to offer two degree programs: Bachelor of Elementary Education and Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.

Vision:

The Binalatongan Community College as source of inspiration, pride, and cornerstone of emerging opportunities in producing 21st Century professionals in the global community.

Mission:

The Binalatongan Community College shall provide advanced technological and professional education leading to an academic degree inculcating in them good values and desirable personality to sustain the purpose-oriented BCCian spirit. It shall develop competitive graduates with transformative knowledge, skills, and attitude in instruction, research, extension and production services.
